About

WaveFocus is a tool to help us implement the Pomodoro Technique, a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s.

The technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.

The goal of the technique is to reduce the impact of internal and external interruptions on focus and flow.

Pomodoro Steps

  • 1. Decide on the task to be done.

  • 2. Start the timer and focus on the task until the alarm setoff.

  • 3. Every completed session, take a short break.

  • 4. Repeat
